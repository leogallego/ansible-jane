Meet Jane

She lives in the network between you and your automation platform. Launch jobs, watch workflows, manage inventories, and ask her anything — all from your phone.

What She Can Do

🤖

Talk to Jane

An AI assistant that speaks Ansible. Ask her to list failing jobs, check host facts, launch a template, or explain what went wrong. She talks to your platform through MCP and 61 local tools, then reports back in plain language.

🚀

Launch & Monitor

Browse job templates, launch playbooks with parameters, watch them run. Approve or deny workflow steps without opening a laptop. See stdout, status, and timing for every job.

🔌

MCP Tools

MCP is Jane's native language. She auto-discovers your AAP's MCP endpoints on connect. Add custom servers for knowledge bases, monitoring, or any tool that speaks the protocol.

📦

Infrastructure at a Glance

Browse inventories, inspect hosts and facts, check groups. 61 local tools cover jobs, inventories, credentials, projects, schedules, EDA, and platform config.

⚡

Event-Driven Ansible

Monitor EDA activations and rulebooks. See what's triggering your event-driven automation without switching to a desktop.

🔒

Secure by Default

Tokens encrypted with Android Keystore + Tink. HTTPS-only. No data on third-party servers. Multi-instance support — connect to several AAP deployments from one app.
